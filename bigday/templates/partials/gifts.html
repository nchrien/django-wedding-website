Malala Fund

Nobel Peace Prize laureate Malala Yousafzai's charitable foundation works to break down barriers to girls' education in developing countries. They support local education activists who fight against gender discrimination, work to reduce the impact of war and poverty on education, and campaign for policies to protect girls' and their schooling.

Doctors Without Borders

Médecins Sans Frontières, or Doctors Without Borders, provides medical aid wherever it's needed most, operating in more than 65 countries around the world. They respond to crises ranging from wars to epidemics, providing services such as vaccination, malnutrition treatment, emergency surgery, and mental healthcare.
